Investigating the risk factors of dementia (R Programming)

Performed statistical analysis using R programming on a dataset from the Open Access Series of Imaging Studies (OASIS) dataset.
Investigated the common risk factors of dementia such as Education level and Age

Investigating the effects of hearing loss and hearing aids (R Programming)

Used R Programming to perform statistical analysis on the National health and Aging Trends Study (NHATS) dataset.
Results concluded that Hearing loss leads to poorer memory but the use of Hearing aids can alleviate these negative side-effects
